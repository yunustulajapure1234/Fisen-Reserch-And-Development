EV Charger

At Faisen, we specialize in providing cutting-edge EV charging solutions engineered to deliver rapid, reliable, and safe power to your electric vehicles. Our chargers are designed to exceed industry standards, offering unmatched reliability and performance in fast, efficient charging even in the worst environmental conditions. As no SMPS is used, it is a highly reliable system.

Each EV charger is meticulously designed to deliver fast and reliable power even during voltage fluctuations and other poor power quality conditions.

Key Features:
Advanced Technology: Utilizing state-of-the-art materials and technology, our EV chargers provide high efficiency, ensuring optimal power transfer with high power quality, e.g., very low total harmonic distortion (THD) and unity power factor.

Robust Construction: Built to withstand outdoor conditions, our EV chargers are durable and long-lasting, backed by rigorous quality control measures to ensure the best performance throughout their lifetime.

Easy Integration: Designed for seamless integration into various residential, commercial, and industrial locations, our EV chargers offer flexibility and scalability to meet your specific charging needs.

Environmental Sustainability: By choosing our EV chargers, you're not only investing in sustainable transportation but also contributing to a cleaner, greener future by reducing your carbon footprint.

Comprehensive Support: We provide comprehensive support from initial consultation to installation and ongoing maintenance, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for our clients.
